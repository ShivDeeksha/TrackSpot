The purpose of this website is to facilitate the process of reporting lost items, matching them with found items, and connecting rightful owners with their lost belongings within our campus community.

Anyone within the campus community, including students, faculty, and staff, can use this website to report lost items, submit found items, and claim lost items.

To report a lost item, simply register an account on the website, log in, and fill out the necessary details about the lost item, including a description, location, and any identifying features. You can also upload a picture of the lost item if available.

If you find a lost item, you can upload details about the item, including a description, location found, and any identifying features. You can also upload a picture to help the owner identify the item.

To claim a lost item, you must first register an account on the website and log in. Then, navigate to the item you wish to claim and submit your claim along with answers to any questions uploaded by the item's owner.

Claims are processed by the owner of the lost item. They can review all claims submitted for their item and decide which claimant they believe is the rightful owner based on the answers provided.

After a claim is approved by the item owner, the claimant will receive a notification with the contact information of the item owner. They can then contact the owner directly to arrange for the return of the lost item.

Yes, users can report multiple lost items and submit multiple found items. Simply log in to your account and follow the same process for each item.
